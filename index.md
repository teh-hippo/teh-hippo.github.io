## Apps

### [Mattershot](https://teh-hippo.github.io/mattershot/)

Back up Matter device QR codes as crisp, re-scannable SVG and PNG images. A static,
client-side app that runs entirely in your browser.

[Open app](https://teh-hippo.github.io/mattershot/) &middot; [Source](https://github.com/teh-hippo/mattershot)

### [Five Crowns Scorer](https://teh-hippo.github.io/fivecrowns/)

A minimal, mobile-friendly scorekeeper for the card game Five Crowns. A single static page
with no build step and no dependencies.

[Open app](https://teh-hippo.github.io/fivecrowns/) &middot; [Source](https://github.com/teh-hippo/fivecrowns)
